Welcome to Chalk!

Your personal scheduling assistant

Do you have trouble managing your schedule? Us too!

That's why we made Chalk, a webservice that will create and manage your schedule for you.

Just tell Chalk what you need to get done, give each activity a due date, and it'll generate a suggested schedule for you to follow throughout the week.
